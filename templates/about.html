Uber Santé is a service which allows you to connect with various clinics and book an appointment with professionals.

Patients

  • Registration is as simple as filling out a form and clicking Submit! Made a mistake? No worries, your information can be corrected by the professionals in charge of the web-site.

  • Booking is done through a convenient calendar view to make sure you get the correct appointment slot and plan our your visit easier.

  • Appointments are stored in a shopping cart, which automatically prevents you from booking conflicting times, or booking two annual appointments in the space of a year.

  • Need to book appointments at multiple clinics? No problem, as long as these clinics are using the UberSanté service.

  • Receipts can be saved to a PDF file, to save you the trouble of printing out a webpage.

Nurses

  • Nurses are the system administrators of UberSanté, booking appointments, adjusting schedules and modifying profile information.

Doctors

  • Making a weekly schedule uses a calendar view and draggable appointments, saving time for more important things, such as seeing patients.

  • Need to modify a single week? The system supports that too, simply adjust the week's schedule without affecting the rest of the schedule.
